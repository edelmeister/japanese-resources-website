Hiragana, Katakana & Kanji Resources

Written Japanese consists of three writing systems. Hiragana and katakana are both a phonetic alphabet. Kanji are chinese characters that represent things, ideas or concepts. You can learn more through the links below.

If you are a beginner, it's recommended to start by learning hiragana and katakana. Once you can read them both comfortably, you can move onto learning the kanji characters.
