Practice Resources

This page contains various resources for practicing Japanese.

Click on any image to open the resource in a new tab.

NHK - the national broadcasting company of Japan - has a website with lessons and practice material for Japanese language learners.

NHK also has news in easy Japanese. Reading these easy news is great practice for recognizing kanji characters and reading comprehension.

Easy Japanese - aka TODAI - is a website and a smartphone app with news from various news organizations in Japan and in Japanese. What makes it great, is that it often includes community translations for articles. It also highlights words by their JLPT level, which you can tap on for quick definitions. Registering for the service also gives you the ability to save vocabulary.

Tadoku has free books graded by level. It's a useful service for getting comprehensible input through reading simple free books.
