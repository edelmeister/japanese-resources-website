Welcome!

This website is intended as a resource bank for Japanese language learners. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner, I hope to provide some help and guidance in your learning journey.

What?

Resources are compiled into three categories, which you can see at the top of the page.

The 'grammar' page contains external resources for learning Japanese grammar.

The 'hiragana, katakana and kanji' page includes an introduction to the three writing systems used in Japanese. Additionally, it lists external resources for learning and memorizing kanji characters.

Finally, the 'exercises' page includes external resources for practicing listening, reading and writing.
